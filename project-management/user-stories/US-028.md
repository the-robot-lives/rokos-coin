# US-028: Delegate my staked ROKO to another miner

**As a** DeFi investor and token holder,
**I want to** delegate my staked ROKO to a trusted miner's stake pool,
**So that** I can earn delegation rewards without running hardware myself, while also strengthening the staking security of miners I want to support.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] A delegation function records a delegator-to-miner relationship and adds the delegated amount to the miner's effective stake for reward calculations
- [ ] Delegated tokens remain in the delegator's address — they are not transferred to the miner; only the staking weight is attributed
- [ ] Delegation can be redirected to a different miner or withdrawn at any time, subject to a short re-delegation cooldown to prevent stake-hopping attacks
- [ ] If the delegated miner is slashed, the delegator's stake is not slashed — only the miner's own stake is at risk (or define a partial delegation risk model in notes)
- [ ] Delegator's share of the miner's rewards is calculated and claimable separately from the miner's direct earnings

## Notes
Whether delegator stake is slashable alongside the miner is a critical design decision with significant economic implications. Keeping delegator stake safe (miner-only slash) encourages delegation but reduces miner accountability from delegators; shared slash risk increases skin-in-the-game alignment. Document the chosen model explicitly in tokenomics.
