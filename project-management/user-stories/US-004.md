# US-004: Stake ROKO as collateral to activate miner status

**As a** independent miner and compute provider,
**I want to** stake a required amount of ROKO tokens as collateral,
**So that** I am economically committed to honest behavior and my miner status is activated for receiving inference job assignments.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Staking contract accepts ROKO tokens and locks them, updating the miner's on-chain status to "active" once the minimum threshold is met
- [ ] The minimum stake amount is readable from a contract constant or governance-controlled parameter
- [ ] Staked balance is visible on the miner dashboard and on the block explorer via the contract's public state
- [ ] Partial stakes below the minimum do not activate miner status but are held and allow top-up without re-staking from zero
- [ ] A Staked event is emitted with miner address and amount, enabling off-chain indexers to track stake changes

## Notes
Collateral is at risk of slashing (US-008). Stake should be locked in the contract, not just tracked by balance. Consider a grace period before newly staked miners receive job assignments to prevent gaming.
