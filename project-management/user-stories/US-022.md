# US-022: View miner leaderboard ranked by inference quality

**As a** independent miner and compute provider,
**I want to** see a public leaderboard of miners ranked by inference quality score, uptime, and total jobs served,
**So that** I can benchmark my performance against the network, identify optimization opportunities, and see where I stand competitively.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Leaderboard displays top N miners ranked by a composite quality score (accuracy, uptime, response latency)
- [ ] Each row shows: rank, miner address (truncated), hardware tier, quality score, jobs served (30d), and total ROKO earned
- [ ] My own miner row is highlighted or pinned regardless of rank when I am logged in with a staked wallet
- [ ] Leaderboard is filterable by hardware tier and sortable by individual metrics
- [ ] Rankings update at least once per epoch and the last-updated timestamp is displayed

## Notes
Quality score methodology should be documented and verifiable from on-chain data. Leaderboard is also a community engagement and marketing tool — consider a public shareable URL per miner profile. Protect against sybil gaming where one entity registers many wallets to dominate the leaderboard visually.
