# US-023: Receive bonus rewards for early network participation

**As a** independent miner and compute provider,
**I want to** receive enhanced reward multipliers during the early network bootstrapping phase,
**So that** I am compensated for the higher risk of joining before the network achieves liquidity and demand, and the protocol incentivizes rapid early miner recruitment.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] An early-participant bonus multiplier is applied to all rewards earned before a defined block height or calendar date
- [ ] Bonus tiers are published pre-launch (e.g., genesis miners, first 30 days, first 90 days) with specific multiplier values and cutoffs
- [ ] Bonus multiplier is applied automatically by the reward contract — miners do not need to claim a separate bonus
- [ ] Early participant status is permanently recorded on-chain as a badge/flag on the miner record, even after the bonus period ends
- [ ] Bonus reward amounts are itemized separately in earnings history (US-006) so miners can verify they received their correct early-participant benefit

## Notes
Early participation bonuses accelerate supply-side bootstrapping but must be funded from the protocol treasury allocation, not new emissions that dilute all holders. Define a hard cap on total bonus ROKO to prevent abuse if the bonus period can be gamed.
