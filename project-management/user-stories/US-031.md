# US-031: Participate in the initial token distribution event

**As a** DeFi investor and token holder,
**I want to** participate in the initial ROKO token distribution event (IDO, airdrop, or fair launch),
**So that** I can acquire ROKO at the genesis price and become an early stakeholder in the network.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Distribution event type, dates, allocation caps, and eligibility criteria are published at least 2 weeks before the event opens
- [ ] Participants can connect their wallet, verify eligibility, and commit their allocation within the event window
- [ ] Anti-whale caps (max per-wallet allocation) are enforced on-chain to promote broad distribution
- [ ] KYC/geo-restriction requirements (if any) are clearly disclosed in advance and enforced before wallet registration, not at token claim time
- [ ] All unsold or unclaimed allocations are returned to the protocol treasury or burned per a pre-disclosed policy, not silently retained by the team

## Notes
The fairness and transparency of the initial distribution event heavily shapes community trust and token holder loyalty. Consider a batch auction or Dutch auction mechanism over a fixed-price sale to achieve price discovery. Sybil resistance for airdrop/whitelist is critical — require wallet age, on-chain activity history, or proof-of-personhood.
