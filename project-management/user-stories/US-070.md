# US-070: Delegate My Voting Power to Another Address

**As a** DeFi investor and token holder,
**I want to** delegate my staked ROKO voting power to a trusted representative address,
**So that** governance participation remains high even when I am unable to monitor and vote on every proposal.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Token holder can delegate to any address (including themselves to self-activate voting) via a single on-chain transaction
- [ ] Delegation takes effect at the next block and applies to all future proposal snapshots until changed
- [ ] Delegator retains full token ownership and staking rewards; only voting rights are transferred
- [ ] Delegatee's total voting power is the sum of all delegated balances and their own staked balance, visible on the governance dashboard
- [ ] Delegator can revoke or change delegation at any time; change takes effect immediately for proposals whose snapshot has not yet been taken

## Notes
Follows ERC-20Votes / ERC-20VotesComp delegation model. Consider meta-transaction support (EIP-2612 permit) so users can delegate without holding ETH/native gas. Display delegation chain clearly in the UI to prevent confusion.
