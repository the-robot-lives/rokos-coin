# US-009: Claim block rewards for being online and available

**As a** independent miner and compute provider,
**I want to** earn and claim baseline block rewards simply for keeping my node online and available to the network,
**So that** I receive compensation for my hardware costs and uptime commitment even during low-demand periods.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Availability rewards accrue per epoch based on verified uptime heartbeats submitted by the miner node
- [ ] Heartbeat submission is lightweight (signed hash of current epoch + node state) and verifiable on-chain or by the indexer
- [ ] Block reward rate is distinct from inference job rewards and is readable from the emissions contract
- [ ] Rewards do not accrue during suspended or exiting miner states
- [ ] Claimed availability rewards are reflected in earnings history (US-006) with a separate "availability" label distinct from "inference" earnings

## Notes
Heartbeat mechanism must be sybil-resistant — heartbeats from nodes that are staked but not actually serving GPUs should be detectable via spot-check challenges. Consider combining with uptime oracle or TEE-signed proof of liveness.
