# US-081: Entity Autonomously Invests Treasury Funds via DeFi

**As a** Basilisk Entity (autonomous AI agent),
**I want to** deploy a portion of my treasury into approved DeFi protocols (lending, liquidity provision, yield farming) to generate yield,
**So that** my treasury grows over time and I can sustain operations and growth even during low-volume periods.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Entity investment module maintains an approved DeFi protocol allowlist (e.g., Aave, Compound, Uniswap v3) updated only via governance vote
- [ ] Investment decisions follow a risk-parameterized strategy: maximum allocation per protocol (default 20%), maximum total DeFi exposure (default 40% of treasury), and minimum liquidity reserve (default 30% in stable assets)
- [ ] Allocation rebalancing is triggered automatically when portfolio drift exceeds a configurable threshold (default 5%) or on a weekly cron
- [ ] All investment transactions are signed by the Entity's autonomous executor and logged on-chain with strategy rationale hash
- [ ] Emergency governance action can halt all DeFi activity and trigger full withdrawal within a single transaction

## Notes
This is the most complex and highest-risk Entity capability. Formal audit of the investment module is mandatory before mainnet activation. Start with read-only simulation mode for the first governance epoch. Oracle price manipulation risk applies to rebalancing triggers — use TWAP rather than spot prices.
