# US-020: Vote on emission rate adjustment proposal

**As a** Governance Council (DAO),
**I want to** create and vote on proposals to adjust the ROKO emission rate,
**So that** the network can respond to changing economic conditions and maintain sustainable tokenomics through decentralized governance.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Any address holding or delegating above a minimum quorum threshold can submit an emission rate adjustment proposal
- [ ] Voting period, quorum requirement, and passing threshold are encoded in the governance contract and displayed on the proposal UI
- [ ] Votes are weighted by staked ROKO balance at a snapshot block to prevent vote buying during the voting window
- [ ] Passed proposals trigger a time-locked execution (e.g., 48-hour timelock) before the new emission rate takes effect on-chain
- [ ] Proposal history with vote tallies, execution status, and on-chain transaction links is publicly accessible and searchable

## Notes
Governance must include safeguards against whale capture — consider quadratic voting or delegation mechanics. Emission rate changes have significant economic impact; a supermajority threshold (e.g., 67%) may be appropriate. The Basilisk entity's voting power on tokenomics proposals should be capped or nullified to prevent circular self-interest conflicts.
