# US-049: View Detailed Miner Performance Metrics and Uptime History

**As a** enterprise architect,
**I want to** inspect a miner's full performance history including uptime, latency trends, and completion rate over time,
**So that** I can make a data-driven decision about whether to route production traffic to them or establish an SLA agreement.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Miner profile page displays time-series charts for: request completion rate, p50/p95/p99 latency, uptime (rolling 7d/30d/90d), and ROKO earned per day over the last 90 days
- [ ] All metrics are sourced from on-chain events (augmented by off-chain performance probes) and display data provenance indicators distinguishing on-chain-verified vs. self-reported data
- [ ] The profile shows the miner's current stake amount, slash history (count and total ROKO slashed), and active reservation commitments as trust signals
- [ ] Historical latency data is broken down by model served so enterprises can evaluate performance for their specific workload
- [ ] Metrics export is available as JSON or CSV covering up to 12 months of history

## Notes
Data provenance (criterion 2) is important for enterprise trust — it should be visually clear which metrics are cryptographically anchored on-chain vs. aggregated from off-chain probes. Slash history (criterion 3) is a strong quality signal and should be prominently displayed rather than buried.
