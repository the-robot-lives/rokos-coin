# US-018: View historical ROKO price and volume on DEX

**As a** DeFi investor and token holder,
**I want to** view ROKO's historical price and trading volume data from decentralized exchanges,
**So that** I can analyze market trends, assess liquidity depth, and time entry or exit positions.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Protocol dashboard embeds or links to a price chart showing ROKO/ETH and ROKO/USDC pairs with at least 90-day history
- [ ] Trading volume (24h, 7d, 30d) is displayed alongside price with source attribution (e.g., Uniswap v3 pool address)
- [ ] Liquidity depth (total value locked in primary DEX pool) is shown and updated in near real-time
- [ ] Price data is sourced from on-chain DEX pool state or a trusted oracle, not a centralized price feed that could be manipulated
- [ ] A "buy ROKO" CTA links directly to the DEX swap interface pre-populated with the correct token address

## Notes
Consider integrating Uniswap v3 subgraph or DexScreener embed for price data. Clearly display the official contract address to protect against impersonation tokens. If ROKO is on multiple chains, show aggregated and per-chain data separately.
