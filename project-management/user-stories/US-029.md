# US-029: Receive delegation rewards as a delegatee miner

**As a** independent miner and compute provider,
**I want to** earn additional rewards from delegated stake attributed to my node,
**So that** I have an incentive to build a strong reputation, attract delegators, and run a high-quality operation that benefits from network-effect capital.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] My effective stake for reward calculation includes delegated amounts, increasing my reward multiplier or share of network rewards proportionally
- [ ] A configurable commission rate (set by me, visible to delegators) determines what percentage of delegation-boosted rewards I retain vs. pass through to delegators
- [ ] Commission rate changes require a notice period (e.g., 3 epochs) before taking effect, protecting delegators from sudden fee increases
- [ ] Dashboard (US-006) shows total delegated stake, number of delegators, my commission rate, and earnings from delegation separately
- [ ] Delegation-derived rewards accrue in the same claimable pool as direct mining rewards and are withdrawable via the same flow (US-027)

## Notes
Commission rate mechanics are key to marketplace competitiveness among miners. Lower commission attracts more delegators; higher commission maximizes per-unit reward at lower delegation volume. Transparent commission history builds trust. Consider a maximum commission cap (e.g., 20%) set by governance to protect delegators.
