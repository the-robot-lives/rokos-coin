# US-077: View Governance Participation Metrics and Voter Turnout

**As a** Governance Council member,
**I want to** access a governance analytics dashboard showing participation rates, voter turnout, and delegation patterns over time,
**So that** the DAO can identify disengagement trends and take corrective action to maintain legitimacy.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Dashboard displays: total eligible voters (addresses with staked ROKO), average turnout per proposal, unique voters per epoch, and trend lines over 30/90/180-day windows
- [ ] Delegation network visualization shows top 20 delegates by total delegated power and their historical voting record
- [ ] Per-proposal breakdown shows turnout percentage, time distribution of votes (early vs. last-minute), and whether quorum was met before the deadline
- [ ] Metrics are computed from indexed on-chain events; data refreshes within 5 minutes of each new vote transaction
- [ ] Export functionality provides a CSV of all governance events for off-chain analysis

## Notes
Low turnout (<10%) on consecutive proposals should trigger an automated alert to governance newsletter subscribers. Consider "governance health score" as a single composite metric. Voter apathy is a known attack surface — this dashboard is a proactive mitigation tool.
